Problem we noticed...

In today's bustling event industry, the problem of understaffing has long affected event venues and businesses in the HoReCa (Hotel, Restaurant, and Catering) sector. The demand for skilled and reliable hospitality professionals often surpasses the available workforce, leading to event organizers and businesses struggling to meet the service standards expected by their clients. Event planning and management should be a seamless experience for all involved, but the chronic shortage of qualified staff often leads to stress, service quality issues, and missed business opportunities. Event venues and HoReCa establishments find themselves in a constant struggle to find temporary or permanent staff on short notice, resulting in added operational costs and lost revenue potential.

What we offer!

HorecaX has recognized this persistent problem and is committed to revolutionizing the event industry. Our innovative platform bridges the gap by seamlessly connecting event venues and HoReCa businesses with a dynamic pool of highly skilled, on-demand hospitality professionals. Through our solution, we aim to eliminate the perennial staffing challenges, ensuring that events run smoothly, service quality remains uncompromised, and businesses can fully capitalize on their growth potential. HorecaX promises to provide a sustainable and efficient solution to the long-standing staffing problem, empowering the event industry to thrive and prosper.

Customer segment

  1. Event Venues: This segment includes various event venues such as banquet halls, conference centers, hotels, and other event spaces. Event venues often face challenges related to staffing, especially during peak event seasons or when dealing with large-scale events. HorecaX can help them find skilled hospitality professionals to ensure the seamless execution of events.
  2. HoReCa Businesses: HoReCa stands for Hotel, Restaurant, and Catering businesses. This segment includes restaurants, bars, cafes, catering companies, and hotels. These businesses frequently require additional staff during busy periods, special events, or to cover for regular employees' leave. HorecaX offers a solution for finding experienced and temporary staff when needed.
  3. Event Planners and Organizers: Event planning and management companies can benefit from HorecaX by accessing a pool of trained hospitality professionals to ensure the success of their events. This segment values reliable and skilled staff to enhance their event services.
  4. Hospitality Professionals: On the other side, your platform caters to hospitality professionals seeking temporary or permanent positions in the event industry. This could include waitstaff, bartenders, chefs, event coordinators, and other skilled individuals who want flexibility in their work arrangements.
  5. Businesses Hosting Corporate Events: Companies hosting corporate events, seminars, or conferences may also be a customer segment. They may need additional staff to ensure that these events run smoothly and professionally.
  6. Entrepreneurs and Investors: Potential investors and entrepreneurs interested in the event industry and staffing solutions might also be part of your customer segment if they see an opportunity to invest in or partner with HorecaX.

Competition

  1. hipo.ro is a popular job search and career platform in Romania. It provides a wide range of job listings across various industries and sectors, making it a go-to destination for both job seekers and employers. Job seekers can search for job listings, create profiles, and receive job alerts. Employers can post job vacancies, search for candidates, and access a database of potential hires. hipo.ro primarily focuses on the Romanian job market, serving both local and international companies operating in Romania.
  2. eJobs.ro is one of the largest and most well-known job search platforms in Romania. It offers a comprehensive array of job listings in various fields, making it a valuable resource for job seekers and employers. Job seekers can search for jobs, create profiles, and receive notifications for new job opportunities. Employers can post job listings and manage their recruitment process using the platform's tools. eJobs.ro focuses on the Romanian job market, providing a platform for local and international companies to find and hire talent in Romania.
  3. Tura9 is a one-day job application that offers money on time. It is suitable for people who want to supplement their income or who want to fill their free time. Users discover cafes and restaurants in their area and can sign up to lend a helping hand, for a fee. The application provides shifts both for people with culinary skills and for those who want to make clean.

Why HorecaX?

Compared with the possible competition listed above, HorecaX is focused on horeca sector, which the other platforms just address to. Both hipo.ro and eJobs.ro include job listings for positions within the HoReCa sector, but they are not mainly focused on it, thus the amount of job searching results is not great. As a result, users have to search on multiple platforms to gather a decent number of jobs. This time consuming activities could be avoided by searching exclusively on HorecaX, which displays only horeca jobs! Moreover, Tura9 is focused on offering one-day jobs, while HorecaX has a wide variety of job types: permanent, temporary and seasonal. HorecaX stands out by being horeca sector focused and offering jobs varied in duration.

Key metrics

  1. Job Listings: Track the number of job listings available our platform
  2. User Registrations: Measure how many job seekers and employers have registered on our platform to use our services.
  3. Job Applications: Monitor the number of applications submitted by job seekers, which shows their level of engagement with the available job listings.
  4. Employer Subscriptions: Track the number of subscriptions and their associated revenue.
  5. Job Seeker Engagement: Analyze how often job seekers return to the platform and how long they spend searching for jobs.
  6. Employer Engagement: Evaluate how actively employers use our platform to post job listings, review applications, and manage their hiring processes.
  7. Conversion Rates: Determine the percentage of visitors who register or apply for jobs after visiting our platform.
  8. Time-to-Fill Jobs: Calculate the average time it takes for employers to find suitable candidates for their job listings.
  9. Revenue per Job Posting: Measure the average revenue generated per job posting.
  10. User Feedback and Ratings: Collect user reviews and ratings to assess satisfaction levels and identify areas for improvement.
  11. Geographic Reach: Track the locations where job listings are available and where job seekers and employers are most active.
  12. Competitive Analysis: Compare the number of job listings and user engagement to competitors in the HoReCa job listing space.
  13. Marketing ROI: Measure the return on investment for our marketing efforts, including advertising and promotional campaigns.
  14. Cost of Customer Acquisition: Calculate how much it costs to acquire each job seeker and employer as a customer.
  15. Retention Rate: Assess how many registered users continue to actively use the platform over time.
  16. Customer Support Metrics: Track response times, resolution rates, and user satisfaction with our customer support services.
  17. Partnerships: Monitor the impact of our partnerships with HoReCa businesses or industry associations on job listings and user engagement.
  18. Platform Performance: Ensure that our platform is fast, reliable, and user-friendly to minimize bounce rates and maximize engagement.

Revenue Streams

  1. Job Posting Fees:

    Charge employers fees to post job listings on your platform. Offer different packages with varying features and visibility options.

  2. Subscription Model:

    Offer subscription plans to employers, allowing them to post a certain number of job listings each month at a fixed monthly or annual fee.

  3. Featured Listings:

    Allow employers to pay for featured or promoted job listings, ensuring their positions appear at the top of search results or in prominent sections of your platform.

  4. Resume Access Fees:

    Charge employers for access to your database of job seeker resumes, allowing them to proactively search for and contact potential candidates.

  5. Advertising and Sponsorships:

    Display Ads: Allow businesses in the HoReCa industry to place display ads on your platform or within job listings.

    Sponsorship Opportunities: Partner with HoReCa-related companies for sponsorships, featured profiles, or dedicated sections on your platform.

  6. Referral Programs:

    Affiliate Marketing: Collaborate with other websites, bloggers, or industry influencers and pay them a commission for each successful job posting or subscription referred from their platforms.

  7. Data and Analytics Services:

    Data Insights: Provide data analytics and insights to employers and HoReCa businesses regarding job market trends and salary benchmarks, for a fee.
