What we did?

We knew that we lacked sufficient resources and on-the-ground information to implement a hiring platform in the HoReCa industry. However, rather than outsourcing everything, we decided to take matters into our own hands. We devised a plan to create CVs ourselves and find employers for potential employees.

We crafted a questionnaire to gather information from individuals, assisting them in creating their CVs.

We generated personalized CVs using LaTeX. We chose LaTeX to create a standardized CV template. This could aid in automating CV creation on the future platform.

We attempted to hire individuals for whom we created CVs. If we succeed in finding employers willing to interview our candidates, it means our idea deserves a chance for implementation.

By taking a hands-on approach and creating a proof-of-concept, we aim to demonstrate the viability of our concept and gather valuable insights for the development of our future hiring platform in the HoReCa sector.
