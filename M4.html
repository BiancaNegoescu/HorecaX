User Stories

User Flow: Job Seeker Interaction

Step 1: Sign-Up
Job seeker registers for a HorecaX account using email

Step 2: Profile Creation
Job seeker creates a detailed profile and resume by completing the mandatory fields (including work experience, skills, and preferences within the Horeca industry)

Step 3: Job Search
Job seeker explores job listings using filters

Step 4: Applying to Jobs
Job seeker selects desired jobs and applies by submitting their profile

User Flow: Employer Engagement

Step 1: Employer Sign-In
Employer registers for a HorecaX account using email

Step 2: Job Posting
Employer creates a job posting by completing the mandatory fields (job title, job details, requirements, and description)

Step 3: Accessing Resumes
Employer views comprehensive resumes of job seekers who have applied to their job postings.

Step 5: Exploring Other Employer Announcements
Optional: Employer navigates to view job announcements posted by other employers within the HorecaX platform for industry insights.
