User Stories

User Flow: Job Seeker Interaction

Step 1: Sign-Up
Job seeker registers for a HorecaX account using email

Step 2: Profile Creation
Job seeker creates a detailed profile and resume by completing the mandatory fields (including work experience, skills, and preferences within the Horeca industry)

Step 3: Job Search
Job seeker explores job listings using filters

Step 4: Applying to Jobs
Job seeker selects desired jobs and applies by submitting their profile

User Flow: Employer Engagement

Step 1: Employer Sign-In
Employer registers for a HorecaX account using email

Step 2: Job Posting
Employer creates a job posting by completing the mandatory fields (job title, job details, requirements, and description)

Step 3: Accessing Resumes
Employer views comprehensive resumes of job seekers who have applied to their job postings.

Step 5: Exploring Other Employer Announcements
Optional: Employer navigates to view job announcements posted by other employers within the HorecaX platform for industry insights.

User Persona

Persona 1: Andrei

  • Age: 19 years old
  • Education: Just finished his first University year
  • Current Situation: In need of money
  • Interests:
    • Enjoys nightlife and meeting new people
    • Wants to go on a city break at the end of the summer
  • Challenges:
    • Doesn't know how to find a summer-time job and where to start
    • Wants a reliable application to avoid being deceived by employers

User Story:

Andrei wants to go on a city break at the end of the summer. He doesn't have a job and doesn't know where to look for one. Andrei enjoys nightlife and believes that he'd do an amazing job as a bartender or waiter. He is afraid of being deceived by employers and needs a reliable application.

Persona 2: Mihai

  • Age: 40 years old
  • Occupation: Owns a coffee franchise for a few months
  • Current Situation: Wants to make the coffee franchise more profitable
  • Needs: A barista who is very efficient and friendly

User Story:

Mihai bought a coffee franchise a few months ago, very well located, but it didn't turn out to be profitable. He pays the current barista too much for the job done, dislikes the coffee being served, and wants to hire someone well qualified and friendly who can make better coffee. Mihai wants to find candidates easily, one click away.
